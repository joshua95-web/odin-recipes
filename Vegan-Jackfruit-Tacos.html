Vegan Jackfruit Tacos

Description

Jackfruit Tacos made on a sheet pan with crispy jackfruit “carnitas”, refried beans, pickled red onions, and Mexican slaw.

Ingredients

  • 1 mexican seasoned jackfruit
  • 8 six inch taco shells
  • 1 can vegan refried beans
  • shredded cabbage or lettuce
  • 1 avocado
  • taco sauce
  • pickled onions
  • cilantro

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. On a parchment-lined sheet pan, lay out the tortillas. Spread with a few tablespoons of refried beans. Top some with cheese if you like or keep this vegan.
  3. Divide the jackfruit carnitas among the tortillas and place them in the oven. Bake until heated through and the edges of the tortillas are crispy about 12-15 minutes.
  4. Top with cabbage slaw ( or shredded lettuce) and avocado, pickled onions and salsa or hot sauce. Serve with optional sour cream or vegan-adaptable chipotle Mayo.
  5. Fold up into tacos and eat immediately!
